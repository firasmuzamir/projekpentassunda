01 Project · Food Aid

Rice, oil & fish
for 300 families.

Coastal villages outside Banda Aceh still struggle with food security after a hard fishing season. We pack 300 family boxes — rice, cooking oil, dry fish, sugar, baby formula — and hand-deliver them village by village.

02 Project · Clean water

One village well,
built to last decades.

Why Aceh, why now

Small trip, honest reporting, real receipts.

We're a team of five volunteers from Selangor. Every ringgit is logged, every box is photographed, every well GPS pin is published. No overhead — flights and food on us.

01

Local partners on the ground

We coordinate with the village imam & school heads weeks in advance — no parachute aid.

02

100% of donations to projects

The five of us cover flights, fuel and food personally. Your gift buys rice, not laptops.

03

Public receipts after the trip

A 20-page photo report goes out by end-July. Every line item, every village, every face.

04

Built on trust, not headlines

This is our second trip. The first served Mae Sot in 2024 — ask us for that report any time.
